## About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the [Australian Artificial Intelligence Institute (AAII)](https://www.uts.edu.au/research/australian-artificial-intelligence-institute), University of Technology Sydney. I completed my Ph.D. at UTS in February 2026, advised by [Prof. Ling Chen](https://profiles.uts.edu.au/Ling.Chen). I have also been fortunate to learn from [A/Prof. Meng Fang](https://mengfn.github.io/).

I study AI agents, from RL agents in game environments to today’s LLM agents in real-world settings. I see these agents as systems that bridge humans and the world, and my goal is to make them both **intelligent** and **trustworthy**.
